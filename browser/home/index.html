ABOUT US

Best Online Quran Academy

Noorulfurqan Academy is an educational platform specialized in teaching the Holy Quran and understanding its meanings in an innovative and simplified way that suits everyone.

The academy was established 5 years ago, and we succeeded in attracting 70% of our students through personal lessons that have evolved to provide integrated educational services via the Internet.

We rely on the TeacherOn platform, Facebook ads, and personal recommendations in our communication with customers, and we provide a distinguished educational experience through groups dedicated to each student to provide daily support, performance reports, and all educational resources.

We aim to attract students through introductory videos and customer experiences, in addition to free lectures during religious occasions.

Despite the challenges of competing with traditional academies, we are committed to our mission of spreading the teachings of the Holy Quran to everyone who aspires to learn it, while taking into account the financial circumstances of our customers and providing a high-quality educational experience.
